Ticket Journey

What happens next

Once you submit your IT ticket, it is logged, reviewed, and updated as it moves through the support process. You may also receive an email update when your ticket status changes.

Step 1 — Ticket Submitted Your issue is logged and assigned a unique support reference number.
Logged
Step 2 — In Progress Once support starts working on your issue, the ticket can be marked as In Progress.
In Progress
Step 3 — Waiting on Support If needed, your issue can be escalated to the relevant head office or support team.
Escalated
Step 4 — Completed or Closed When the issue has been resolved, the ticket is marked as completed or closed and you can be notified by email.
Resolved
